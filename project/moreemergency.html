Stages Of Labor And To Expect

First Stage of Labor is divided into three phases:

Early Labor
  • Mild, irregular contractions (30-45 seconds)
  • Cervix begins to efface and dilate (3 cm)
  • Lasts hours to several days
    • Active Labor
  • Intense, regular contractions (3-5 minutes, 45-60 seconds).
  • Cervix dilates to 4-7 centimeters
  • Transition is the most intense, painful phase
    • Transition Stage
  • Strong contractions (60-90 seconds, 2-5 minutes apart).
  • Cervix fully dilates (10 cm)
  • Emotionally and physically challenging, but the shortest first stage phase.

    Heavy Vaginal Bleeding

  • Seek immediate medical attention. While waiting for help, lie down with your feet elevated, keep warm, and do not use tampons.

    • Intense, persistent abdominal pain

  • Seek emergency medical care immediately, as severe abdominal pain can indicate a variety of serious issues, including ectopic pregnancy or placental abruption

    • A noticeable decrease in the baby's movements

  • Contact your healthcare provider or go to the hospital for an evaluation. A change in fetal movement can be a sign of distress.

    • Sudden, severe swelling of the hands, face, or legs

  • Contact your healthcare provider, as this can be a sign of preeclampsia or another medical issue.

    Mehato ea Mosebetsi le Ho e Lebella

    Mokhahlelo oa pele oa mosebetsi o arotsoe ka mekhahlelo e meraro:

    Mosebetsi Oa Pele Oa Bokhachane
  • Mafutho a bonolo, a sa tsitsang (metsotsoana e 30-45)
  • Mlomo oa popelo o qala ho fifala le ho atoloha (3 cm)
  • E nka lihora ho isa matsatsing a 'maloa
    • Mosebetsi o Mahlahahlaha
  • Ho honyela ho matla, ho tlwaelehileng (metsotso e 3-5, metsotsoana e 45-60).
  • Mlomo oa popelo e hola ho fihla ho 4-7 centimeters
  • Phetoho ke mohato o matla ka ho fetisisa, o bohloko ka ho fetisisa
    • Mokhahlelo oa Phetoho
  • Ho honyela ho matla (60-90 metsotsoana, metsotso e 2-5 ho arohana).
  • Mlomo oa popelo e hola ka botlalo (10 cm)
  • Ho na le phephetso maikutlong le mmeleng, empa mohato wa pele o mokgutshwane ka ho fetisisa.

    Ho tsoa Mali ho Boholo ka Bosaling

  • Batla thuso ya bongaka hanghang. Ha u ntse u emetse thuso, paqame u phahamisitse maoto, u futhumale, 'me u se ke ua sebelisa lipompo.

    • Bohloko bo matla, bo phehellang ka mpeng

  • Batla thuso ea bongaka ea tšohanyetso hang-hang, kaha bohloko bo boholo ka mpeng bo ka bontša mathata a fapaneng a tebileng, ho kenyelletsa le ectopic pregnancy kapa placental abruption

    • Ho fokotseha ho hlokomelehang ha ho sisinyeha ha lesea

  • Ikopanye le mofani wa hao wa tlhokomelo ya bophelo kapa o ye sepetlele bakeng sa tlhahlobo. Phetoho ea ho sisinyeha ha lesea e ka ba pontšo ea mahlomola.

    • Ka tšohanyetso, ho ruruha ho matla ha matsoho, sefahleho, kapa maoto

  • Ikopanye le mofani wa hao wa tlhokomelo ya bophelo, kaha sena e ka ba sesupo sa preeclampsia kapa bothata bo bong ba bongaka.
