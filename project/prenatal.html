Prenatal Care

What is Prenatal Care ?

Prenatal Care is the medical attention and support a pregnant women recieves from healthcare professionals.
Prenatal Care enusres that the mother and her baby stay healthy during pregancy .

Who Provides Prenatal Care

Prenatal care is provided by medical professionals such as
Doctors , nurses and midwifes

Benefits Of Early And Consistent Prenatal Care

Monitoring and detection of complications
Promotion of a healthy pregnancy
Reduced risk of preterm birth
Emotional and psychological support

Cultural Considerations In Lesotho For Prenatal Care

Recognizing and valuing the way things are done in the community is very important.
This includes understanding and respecting local traditions, ceremonies, and the people who help during childbirth in traditional ways.
It's also crucial to include family members and community leaders in decisions about prenatal care and to get advice from older, respected members of the community.
All of this helps make sure that the prenatal care provided respects and fits with the local culture.
